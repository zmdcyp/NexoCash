How We Use Your Permissions:

📍 Location Services

We use approximate (coarse) location data to identify your general neighborhood. This helps us refine our credit modeling and strengthen our anti-fraud systems.

📱 Installed Application Data

NexoCash analyzes the list of apps installed on your device to help us assess creditworthiness and detect potential loan-related fraud. This data is securely uploaded to our private servers.

📷 Camera Access

Camera access is required exclusively for the KYC (Know Your Customer) process. You will use it to capture and upload photos of your ID and other necessary documents during your application.

📅 Calendar Integration

To help you maintain a healthy credit history, we sync with your calendar to provide repayment reminders and bill alerts. We only access "financial-only" calendar events—specifically those related to loans and payments.
